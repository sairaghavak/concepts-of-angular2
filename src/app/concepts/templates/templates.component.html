Templates

  • A template in angular2 is just another html page.
  • Templates are bind to components. The term binding is commonly used in most of the web development frameworks wherein a html element is bind to a property in the backing bean or a class. In the Data Binding section, I will describe more about this concept.
  • Templates can have other templates inside them. This can be done by just adding the selector property value that is defined in a component.
  • As an analogy, we can think of Scala templates in Play framework and also, Expression Language in JEE.
